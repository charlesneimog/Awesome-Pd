    Home

    Under development

    WebSite that tries to list, with descriptiors, music examples, and links, all the objects, externals, and libraries available for PureData.

    Add missing objects or libraries

    Please, if you miss any object, external, or library, or you have any suggestion, tell us in this issue.

    Objects

    • AI - related objects.

    • Reverb - reverb objects.
    • Spatialization - ambisonics and binaural objects.
    • Synthesis - synthesis objects.
